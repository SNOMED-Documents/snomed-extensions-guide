# 5.6.1 Preparing for Distribution

Before an extension is ready for distribution it needs to be classified (if distributed as an edition), validated and packaged properly. Extension producers also need to ensure their content is aligned with the International Edition of SNOMED CT. The following pages explore several key aspects of preparing a SNOMED CT edition for distribution, including:
